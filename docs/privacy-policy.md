# Privacy Policy for PowerSync Mobile

**Last updated: December 24, 2025**

## Overview

PowerSync Mobile ("the App") is designed to help you monitor and manage your Tesla Powerwall battery system. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device
- Tesla Gateway credentials (stored securely in device keychain)
- Home Assistant connection settings
- Flask server connection settings
- Battery health scan results
- App preferences and settings

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Network Communications

The App communicates directly with:
- Your Tesla Powerwall Gateway (local network only)
- Your Home Assistant instance (if configured)
- Your self-hosted Flask server (if configured)
- Amber Electric API (for electricity pricing, if configured)

All communications are initiated by you and data remains on your devices or your self-hosted servers.

## Data Security

- Sensitive credentials are stored using iOS Keychain (encrypted storage)
- Local network communications use the protocols provided by Tesla Gateway
- No data is transmitted to our servers

## Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.

## Your Rights

You can delete all stored data by uninstalling the App from your device.
