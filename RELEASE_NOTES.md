## What's Changed

**Optimizer windows now show energy and estimated value**
Planned Battery Windows on the PowerSync dashboard now show the battery energy scheduled for each charge, discharge, or export window, together with estimated charging cost or export earnings. The estimates integrate the actual interval-by-interval power and tariff prices, so variable-power plans, merged windows with gaps, and negative prices are represented accurately instead of being inferred from the displayed peak power.

Update available via HACS
