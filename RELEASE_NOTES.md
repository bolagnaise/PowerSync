## What's Changed

**Powerwall pack labels no longer assume PW3**
PowerSync no longer labels leader and follower battery-pack health cards as PW3 by default. Sites with Powerwall 2 packs that expose leader/follower-style health data now show neutral Powerwall labels instead of implying the batteries are Powerwall 3 units, while expansion-pack labels remain distinct.
