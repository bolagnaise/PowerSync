## What's Changed

**Provider portal login is now its own Configure page**
GloBird and Flow Power portal login has moved out of the Electricity pricing & provider form and into a new `Provider portal login` page in the PowerSync Configure menu. This keeps tariff and pricing-model settings separate from account login, SMS verification, and portal credential refreshes.

**Cleaner provider pricing forms**
The GloBird and Flow Power pricing pages no longer include the extra “open portal login section” toggle. Users can now update tariff, AEMO, PEA, and network settings without being routed through account login, while still having a dedicated page for the portal-backed account sensors.
