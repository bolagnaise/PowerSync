## What's Changed

**Free-price charge slots now remain active for their full duration**
Smart Optimization keeps the inverter in charge mode throughout a genuinely free import slot, even after the battery reaches its configured grid-charge cap or physical full state. Battery energy, state-of-charge, grid-flow, cost, and savings calculations remain limited to the energy the battery can actually accept.
