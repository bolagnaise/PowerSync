## What's Changed

**Day summaries now discard a single stale daily total**
PowerSync now recognizes early-morning recorder reset skew even when only one energy field still carries yesterday's value. This prevents the mobile Day summary from showing yesterday's solar generation beside today's import, export, battery, and home totals, while retaining recorder history for a genuinely fresh accumulator.
