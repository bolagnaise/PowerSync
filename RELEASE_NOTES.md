## What's Changed

**Dedicated provider portal login sections**
GloBird and Flow Power options now keep portal account login in separate sections from tariff and pricing-model settings. The provider settings screens explain what each section controls, then route users into the portal credentials flow only when they choose to connect or refresh the account login.

**Clearer GloBird and Flow Power setup copy**
The new section descriptions call out which sensors depend on portal access, including GloBird balance, invoice, usage, cost, and ZeroHero readiness sensors, plus Flow Power PEA, LWAP, TWAP, DLF, and demand metrics. Credential and SMS verification prompts now use provider-specific titles and descriptions so the login process is easier to follow.
