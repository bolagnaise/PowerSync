## What's Changed

**Remove saved Custom TOU periods**
The shared AGL Battery Rewards and Custom TOU editor now lists a removal choice for every explicit tariff period. Removing a selected period saves the reduced schedule immediately, so accidental or obsolete entries can be deleted without recreating the tariff.

**Safe editor behaviour**
The editor keeps all periods by default, ignores stale or invalid removal selections without adding a replacement row, and continues to generate the off-peak schedule for any remaining uncovered hours.
