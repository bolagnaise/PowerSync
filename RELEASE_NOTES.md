## What's Changed

**Average Cost per kWh — New Energy Metric**
Two new sensors show your effective electricity rate after accounting for solar exports: *Average Cost per kWh Today* resets at midnight alongside the existing daily energy stats, while *Average Cost per kWh Month to Date* accumulates across the calendar month. The formula is `(import cost − export earnings) ÷ home consumption` — so a heavy-export day can legitimately show a negative rate, meaning your system earned more than it spent. Both sensors appear automatically in the Daily Cost Tracking tile on the auto-generated dashboard, and the metric is included in the mobile app's energy summary for any requested period.
